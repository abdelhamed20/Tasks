linux:is an open source multi-tasking, multi-user operating system. It was initially developed by Linus Torvalds in 1991.
Linux OS is widely used in desktops, mobiles, mainframes.

Unix:is multi-tasking, multi-user operating system but is not free to use and is not open source.
It was developed in 1969 by Ken Thompson team at AT&T Bell Labs. It is widely used on servers, workstations

comparisons between them:
cost:linux is free to use but unix is licensed.
Usage: Linux is used in wide varieties from desktop, servers, smartphones to mainframes but unix is mostly used on servers,
workstations or PCs.

examples:
linux:Ubuntu, Debian GNU, Arch Linux
Unix:SunOS, Solaris, SCO UNIX, AIX, HP/UX, ULTRIX
